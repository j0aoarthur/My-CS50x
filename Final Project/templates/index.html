Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

A QR code, short for Quick Response code, is a two-dimensional barcode that consists of black squares arranged on a white background. It is designed to be scanned by a smartphone or QR code reader, enabling users to quickly access information or perform actions, such as opening a website, adding contact information, or initiating a payment.
